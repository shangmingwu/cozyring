Adding your site to Cozyring

To add your site to Cozyring, an entry needs to be made for your site in sites.json, and then links to the appropriate next and previous endpoints should be added to your site.

Contact the owner of this Cozyring deployment about adding your site to this ring, and then follow the instructions for your site once it appears in the list below.
