Adding your site to Cozyring

If you'd like to add your site to Cozyring, make a pull request to this repository to add your entry to sites.json.

Then, if I approve your entry, your site will pop up in the main listing and in the below instrucitons list. Click on your entry in the instructions list to see the links you need to add on your end.
